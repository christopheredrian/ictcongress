12th ICT Research Congress
The Northern Luzon ICT Research Congress is an annual event which takes place as part of the Research Month. The School of Computing and Information Sciences is responsible for staging the Research Congress, in which all schools in Northern Luzon are invited to participate. Researches coming from the participants are screened by PSITE according to its theme and appropriateness.
Learn More
12th ICT Research Congress
As this is already the 12th Research Congress to be held by SCIS, there is the opportunity to reduce internal costs and maximize earnings by using the previous Research Congress as reference to optimize the facilitation of the event. The earnings from the proceeds will be used for buying equipment for the school and for investing in quality products.
Learn More

The Northern Luzon ICT Research Congress will help develop and foster creativity, critical thinking and competence in the students by showing them the researches made by the participants, allowing them to compare their researches and get inspiration from the researches created by others.
